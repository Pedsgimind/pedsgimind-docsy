This blog is designed to help families and learners find **clear, organized health information**.

## The easiest ways to find a topic

### 1) Use the left sidebar (Categories)
On the blog page, the left sidebar lists categories. Click one to filter posts by topic.

### 2) Use tags
Many posts include tags (for example: *Nutrition*, *Growth*, *Pediatric Gastroenterology*). Clicking a tag shows related posts.

### 3) Search
If your site search is enabled, use it to quickly find keywords (for example: “constipation”, “reflux”, “celiac”).

## How to read a post

Most posts include:

- A short summary at the top
- Practical “what to do” guidance
- Red flags (when to seek urgent care)
- References or guideline-based points (when relevant)

## Important note

Content here is **general education** and cannot replace a clinician assessing your child. If you are concerned about severe symptoms, dehydration, breathing problems, severe pain, or bleeding, seek urgent medical help.
